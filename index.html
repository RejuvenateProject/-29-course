What You'll Experience in This Taster Course

This self-contained module mirrors the proven 4-step format of our full 6-week program, giving you a genuine sample of the transformative learning experience that awaits you.

Step 1

The Expert Insight: The Window of Tolerance

Discover Dr. Dan Siegel's powerful framework for understanding your nervous system. Learn about the three states that govern your daily emotional and physiological responses:

  • The Optimal Window: Calm, alert, and engaged
  • Hyper-arousal: Fight-or-flight, anxiety, overwhelm
  • Hypo-arousal: Freeze, shutdown, numbness

This simple yet profound language helps you understand and navigate stress with greater awareness.

Step 2

The Reflection: Mapping Your Window

Move from theory to practical self-awareness with guided reflection activities. You'll explore:

  • How you respond to recent stressful events
  • Your personal early warning signs (shallow breathing, tight shoulders, racing thoughts)
  • Patterns of hyper-arousal (anger, panic) or hypo-arousal (feeling zoned out, numb)

This personalized insight helps you recognize when you're nearing the edge of your window.

Step 3

Mindfulness Practice: The 3-Minute Breathing Space

Experience a guided mindfulness exercise that is a cornerstone of stress reduction. This simple, memorable, and effective tool:

  • Supports widening your Window of Tolerance
  • Can be used immediately in daily life
  • Provides instant grounding during moments of stress

Take this powerful practice with you beyond the course.

Step 4

Join the Woman's Circle for Discussion

Experience the power of community support in a safe, moderated online session where you can:

  • Voluntarily share your reflections on the Window of Tolerance
  • Connect with other women on similar journeys
  • Feel the support and understanding that is central to our full program

This is your taste of the transformative community that awaits you.
